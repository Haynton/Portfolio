About

I am currently transitioning into web development, with a strong motivation to learn and grow while building accessible, thoughtfully designed interfaces.
I am particularly interested in the balance between design and technology, where usability and performance play a key role in user experience.
I strongly believe that good web development requires a deep understanding of real user needs and business use cases.

In the past, I had the opportunity to develop websites for my own businesses.
This experience allowed me to explore the field in a practical way and gradually build a solid foundation of hands-on knowledge.
I learned to solve problems independently, from initial concept to deployment, and to turn concrete needs into functional solutions.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading Label 619 comic books and playing dungeon crawler board games or D&D with friends.
